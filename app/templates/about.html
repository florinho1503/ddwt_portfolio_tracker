Julius van den Brekel, Reinout Vrielink, Floris Bokx

This application is a data-driven web application designed to help users manage and monitor their investments in stocks. The application allows users to track the performance of their portfolio in real-time and visualize trends and other other helpful statistics, and receive updates on market changes. By integrating with external stock market APIs, the app provides users with accurate and up-to-date information about their investments.
